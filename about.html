Career Ops · About this project

Built by someone who
lived the problem.

Career Ops is a locally-run AI toolkit for professional job seekers in Australia. It was built to close the gap between what recruiters use and what candidates have access to — with no accounts, no subscriptions, and no data leaving your machine.

JL
Johan Lopez
Creator · Career Ops
Professional immigrant navigating the Australian job market — and frustrated enough to build a better system. Career Ops started as a personal tool and grew into something worth sharing.

The system is designed with a specific person in mind: a qualified professional arriving in Australia with real credentials and real experience, but without local networks, without familiarity with how Australian hiring works, and without the tools that make a real difference.
Why this exists

The Australian job market is not broken — but it is asymmetric. Recruiters and ATS platforms use AI to filter applications before any human ever reads them. Most job seekers, especially those who are new to the market, don't have equivalent tools on their side.

Career Ops is designed to close that gap. It doesn't fabricate experience or game the system. It helps you reframe what you already have in the language that Australian hiring managers are actually screening for — and gives you the structure to apply consistently, track clearly, and improve with each attempt.

It is specifically useful for professional immigrants in Australia who hold overseas qualifications, carry international experience that doesn't translate directly, and are unfamiliar with local CV conventions, industry terminology, or how to position their background for roles here.

⚠ Important Disclaimer

Career Ops is a productivity and drafting tool — not a guarantee of employment outcomes. It assists with CV formatting, language repositioning, and application organisation. It does not apply for jobs on your behalf, contact employers, or guarantee that any generated content will result in interviews or offers.

AI-generated content must be reviewed by you before use. The system uses a third-party AI model (Groq / Llama) to generate suggestions. All outputs should be read critically and edited to accurately represent your actual qualifications and experience. Do not submit content that misrepresents your background.

Your data stays local. Career Ops runs entirely on your own machine. Your CV data, job descriptions, and profile are stored only in config.json and pipeline.json on your computer. The only external connection made is to the Groq API for AI processing — no data is stored, logged, or shared by Career Ops itself.
