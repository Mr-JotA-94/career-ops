The origin

Built by someone who
lived this.

"I was qualified. I had years of experience. I was sending application after application — and hearing nothing back. Not because I was wrong for the roles. Because my CV was being filtered out before anyone read it." — Johan Lopez, Creator · Career Ops

Career Ops started as a personal tool. After going through the Australian job market as a professional immigrant and finding the available tools either generic, expensive, or built for a completely different context — I built something that actually fits the problem.

The system is designed around a specific reality: you have genuine qualifications and real experience, but your CV is formatted for a different market, your credentials don't map directly, and you're competing against people with local networks you haven't had time to build yet.

Career Ops doesn't change who you are. It helps the right people actually see you.

The problem

The playing field
isn't level.

01
ATS filters you out before anyone reads your CV
Applicant Tracking Systems screen CVs automatically. If your keywords, formatting, or section labels don't match what the system expects, you're eliminated before a recruiter sees your name.
02
Australian CV conventions are different
Length, structure, tone, what to include and what to leave out — the expectations are specific to this market. What works in the UK, USA, Latin America, or Asia doesn't always translate directly.
03
Your overseas credentials don't speak for themselves
Degrees, certifications, and job titles from other countries often don't map cleanly to Australian equivalents. Without translation, the value gets lost in the screening process.
04
The local network takes years to build
A significant share of Australian roles are filled through referrals and direct outreach before being publicly advertised. Without that network, you're competing at a structural disadvantage.

Why different

Not another chatbot.
A system.

🔒 Privacy by design
Everything runs on your machine. Your CV, personal details, and job search history are never uploaded, stored, or processed by Career Ops servers — because there are none.
🇦🇺 Built for Australia
Role categories, industry signals, visa considerations, city-specific demand, and Australian CV conventions — all tuned to this market specifically, not adapted from a US or UK template.
🎯 Repositioning, not fabrication
The AI rewrites framing and terminology — never facts. Your achievements, your experience, your voice. The system helps the right people see what you already have.
$0 Genuinely free

Get started

Running in
under 10 minutes.

01
Get a free Groq API key
Go to console.groq.com → API Keys. Free account, no credit card. Takes 2 minutes.
02
Clone the repository
Download Career Ops from GitHub to your computer.
git clone https://github.com/Mr-JotA-94/career-ops.git
03
Install dependencies & start
One install command, then run the server.
npm install && node server.mjs
04
Complete your profile
Open localhost:3131 in your browser. The setup wizard walks you through everything in about 8 minutes. Done once — every tool is personalised from here.
